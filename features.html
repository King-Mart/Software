How To

    Download the Push! app
    Create an account either through our website or through the app
    Select your personal health and fitness goals
    Wear the tracker or place it on the equipment
    Calibrate the sensors by doing a few excercises to your own pace, but keep it consistent
    Let our app create a personalized plan based on your goals and results
    Next time you excercise, turn on the sensors and allow it to automatically track your movements during your excercise
    Get real-time results to see how consistent you performance is compared to your calibration
    Check the details of your progress and workout through the app or through the website
    Get personalized tips so that you get your own coach tailored to your skills and level

It is as simple as that! Push! is your coach in your pocket
