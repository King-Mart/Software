Push Fitness is a startup company with the product "Push".

At Push Fitness, we are passionate about empowering individuals on their fitness journeys. Our team is dedicated to revolutionizing the way you approach workouts with our cutting-edge fitness tracking app, Push.

What Makes Push! Unique

Both an app and web version to check your progress and see how you do. The only difference is that the app is directly connected to the sensors to view real-time data. The website, however, is not directly connected since it is run through the web, but the progress and details of the workout after it is done can be viewed through the website.

Not required to hold onto your phone while you workout. The sensors that track your workout sends data to the app so you do not need to physically hold your phone.

No trackers available? No problem! You can use your phone to act as the sensor by wearing it or attaching your phone to the equipment. Just make sure to turn on the "Tracking/Sensor" feature.

You no longer have to manually keep track of your reps and then input it into the app. The sensors and trackers do it all for you.

Data from the sensors convert your movements and motion into easily-readable and understandble way for humans to understand.
